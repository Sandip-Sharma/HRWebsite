Our Founder

Jill Revard, SPHR, SHRM-SCP, founded Revard164 Consulting with a vision to provide strategic HR solutions that drive business success. With over 15 years of human resources leadership across multiple industries including healthcare, hospitality, manufacturing, and non-profit sectors, Jill brings a wealth of practical expertise to every engagement.

A recognized HR thought leader, Jill maintains active certifications as a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) and SHRM Senior Certified Professional (SHRM-SCP). She is committed to staying at the forefront of employment law changes, workforce trends, and innovative HR practices.
